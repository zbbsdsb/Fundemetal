## Posting cadence & anti-spam notes

- **Don't dump everything at once.** Spread across 5–7 days: HN first (best for technical traction), then Reddit, then X thread, then PH.
- **Engage before you post.** Comment genuinely on 2–3 existing threads in each community a day prior; don't land as a drive-by linker.
- **Tailor, don't copy-paste blindly.** The Reddit r/LocalLLaMA and r/PromptEngineering variants are intentionally different voices — keep them that way.
- **Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours** on HN/Reddit; momentum is everything.
- **PH timing:** launch ~12:01 AM PT for a full day on the front page; have the first comment pre-written.
- **Track with UTM** if you can: append `?utm_source=hn` etc. to `[site-url]` so you know what converts.
